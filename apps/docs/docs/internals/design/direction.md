# Project Direction & Motivation

A home for the _why_ of GoFish — the motivation behind the project and where it is
headed. Unlike the rest of the wiki, this page is deliberately speculative: it records
intent and open directions, not shipped behavior.

## Planned contents

- What GoFish is for: the gap in the charting-library landscape it aims to fill.
- The bet on declarative, composable graphics and a small expressive core.
- Near-term direction — the Python wrapper, the maturing non-Cartesian coordinate work.
- Longer-term, open questions and ideas not yet committed to.

## Notes

Keep this page honest about uncertainty. Move items to a `stable` essay once they ship;
keep genuinely open questions here.
